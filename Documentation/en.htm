Instructions

 

1.       Open NadIR-AHK to run the scrip.

2.       Open NadIR VST plugin to work.

3.       You can close the script on taskbar.

 

Recomendations

 

·         NadIr VST plugin window need the focus to work with the script. If you use Reaper, you have to active floating windows plugin.

·         If you have a window (diferente to plugin) with NadIR title, closse the script to avoid interference. 

·         If the open window to load impulse open again to press OK, only press escape key. 

 

About NadIr.

 

NadIr is a free vst by igniteamps to Mac and Windows of 32 and 64 byts. With this plugin you can load impulses responses to guitar or guitar bass. A impulse response is a audio file (prefer WAV), this file have equalization parameters to emule traditional cabinets of famous models or bands. So if you put Nadir after a distortion plugin, you will get a natural  a exelent sound.

You can get so much impulses responses packs free in the internet.

 

Visit: http://www.igniteamps.com/

 

 

functions.

 

·         Low pass

·         High pass.

·         Paner

·         Valance

·         Delay

·         Mode (mono, doble mono, stereo ).

·         Quality (low, normal, High and extreme).

·         Power 1 (on/off button to channel 1)

·         Power 2 (on/off button to channel 2)

·         Bypass (on/off NadIR button)

 

You can change these parameters from sequeencer like much effects.

 

Loading a impulse response.

 

a.       Press 1 and select your impulse and ready.

b.       If in that folder there are impulses, you can press f to next impulse and press d to go back.

 

Stereo mode.

 

NadIr can create a stereo effects, with it you get a effects like play with two guitars at the same time. Also can select different impulses to right and left.

 

After loaded a impulse to first channel (channel for default), to enable stereo effect, you have to do:

a.       Press m and select stereo.

b.       Press alt+2 to turn on chanel 2.

c.       Press 2 to load the impulse response.

d.       Go to Sonar and in the sequeencer put delay 1 or delay 2 at 20 ms and play your guitar to listen.
